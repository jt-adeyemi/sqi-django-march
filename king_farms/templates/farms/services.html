Our Specializations

At King Farms, we take pride in our diverse operations, managing a full spectrum of livestock categories with precision and care:

  • Beef & Dairy Cattle: Home to a world-class herd of heritage and high-yield breeds.
  • Poultry Division: Large-scale production of broiler chickens and organic, free-range eggs.
  • Aquaculture: Integrated freshwater ponds producing sustainable tilapia and catfish.
